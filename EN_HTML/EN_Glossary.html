Glossaire

Biodiversity

Biodiversity refers to the diversity of living species and their ecosystems, as well as the interactions and role they play in nature. It is crucial for the health of our planet and for our own well-being as a species.

Addiction

Addiction is the state of dependency on a psychoactive substance, such as alcohol, illegal drugs, or prescription medications. This can result in excessive and uncontrolled use of these substances, which can have negative effects on physical and mental health, as well as on the relationships and responsibilities of the addict.

de-stigmatize

To de-stigmatize means to end stigmatization, that is, to the discrimination or shame attached to a person or group of people due to their membership in a certain social group, their health status, their appearance...

Prozac

Prozac is the brand name for a medication called fluoxetine, which is used to treat depression, obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD), panic disorder, and premenstrual dysphoric disorder (PMDD)

Machine learning

Machine learning is a field of artificial intelligence that involves using algorithms and models to allow a machine to learn from data, without being explicitly programmed to perform a specific task.

Indelible

The adjective indelible describes something that is impossible to erase or completely remove.

Recrudescence

A resurgence refers to a sudden increase or a return of an activity, disease, or phenomenon that had decreased or disappeared.
