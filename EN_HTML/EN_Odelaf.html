Music Le cafe

Iconic music by the duo Oldelaf and Monsieur D

The title “Le café” is a song from the Album of Maturity released in 2005. The group is known for humorous songs in the 2000s This music goes on the cafe diatribe with one of the problems of society is that men drink a lot of coffee at work. The song tells us the story of a man during his working day, he begins his day by taking a first coffee when waking up then a second when arriving at the office when no one is there, as the day progresses he drinks more coffee, then several times at the office and back home, and with each new coffee it becomes more and more, until it falls in the absurd with the phrase “Mmh, I throw her out the window, she had been looking for it”.

In this song, the band purposely repeats several times the word coffee (total of 9 times). Their goal is to highlight evidence in a satirical and humorous way, the abuse of consumption of coffee, coffee can cause number effect secondary to its overconsumption such as nervousness, restlessness, anxiety, insomnia, irritation of the stomach. You can see the decadence of man during the music, with his increasingly strong voice accentuating the psychological drift of the character The music follows a faster rhythm as the man sinks into his madness, directly referring to restlessness caused by overconsumption of coffee The music will have had the right to its small success with a clip created from this music by the Angouleme School of Animation Cinema professions accumulate more than 20M seen on youtube featuring the man in his day with small adobe flash type animation, the clip made in 2007 .
