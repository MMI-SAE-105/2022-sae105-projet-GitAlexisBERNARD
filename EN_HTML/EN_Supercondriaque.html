The superchondriac film and a comedy film released in 2014 written and directed by the famous French actor Dany Boon. In this film, he himself is the main actor. He embodying a man aged 40 by the name of Romain Faubert, He is a hypochondriac, having an excessive fear of diseases the film plays around this mental disorder which gives him completely unreal situations (example uses gel hydroalcoholic to disinfect the face of bacteria from a hug) the primary goal is to make the audience laugh.

Actors

Justifying the name of the movie not just being a hypochondriac but supercondric. The film goes beyond its humorous side by highlighting humanitarian aid associations such as Médecin du Monde. Dany boon wanted that his film wanted to make his viewers aware of the need for help from the outside world, taking as the main character someone who has no no serious problem, just someone who creates fakes and who gradually as the film manages to change his and overcame his fear of illnesses. The gap between the scenes going from very luxurious Parisian apartments to places of war allowing this film to give a second message beyond its entertaining side.

Romain Fobert's character and accompanied throughout the film by his doctor Dr Dimitri who is fed up with him and his many visits useless but still decides to help him cure his illness by finding him a woman and by helping her and forcing her to do humanitarian aid he will so gradually evolve by overcoming his illness through places unsanitary like a prison where he will have to live despite his unpleasant feelings who can feel. The film has accumulated more than 6,373,542 seats for its film in the world.
